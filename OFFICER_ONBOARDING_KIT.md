## ðŸ‘® Your Powers & Responsibilities

### 1. Moderation Tools
-   **Mute**: Temporarily stop a user from chatting. Use for spam or minor insults.
-   **Kick**: Remove a user from a room/stream. Use for disruption.
-   **Ban**: Permanently block a user (Requires Lead Officer approval for >24h).
-   **Flag**: Mark content for review without immediate action.

### 2. Escalation Protocol
-   **Level 1 (Minor)**: Spam, mild toxicity. -> **Warn** then **Mute**.
-   **Level 2 (Moderate)**: Harassment, hate speech. -> **Kick** (with fee) or **Temp Ban**.
-   **Level 3 (Severe)**: Illegal content, threats, grooming. -> **Permaban** + **Report to Admin**.

### 3. The "Troll" Philosophy
We are *Troll* City. A little trolling is allowed!
-   **Allowed**: Pranks, sarcasm, competitive banter.
-   **Not Allowed**: Slurs, doxxing, threats, sexualizing minors.
-   **Golden Rule**: "Is it funny for everyone, or just the bully?"
